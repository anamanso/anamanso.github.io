Lithuania

If you ever come to visit the Baltic countries, Trakai should be a must see place in your list. This place, is a city an lake resort located in Lithuania, 28km West from Vilnius. The name Trakai comes from the derivation of many different languages such as German (Tracken) or Polish(Troki) until it derived to Lithuanian (Trakai, sing. of Trakas).

This place in inhabitated by around 5,400 inhabitants and it was founded by people from many different nationalities, which makes it even more attractive to visit due to the historic diversity. Even though it once was very diverse,most of the inhabitants of this city are Lithuanians, followed by Polish and Russians.
